FAQs

Window blinds and treatments are coverings that are used to control the amount of light, privacy, and insulation in a room. They come in various styles and materials, such as blinds, shades, curtains, and drapes.

Consider factors like the room’s decor, your privacy needs, the amount of natural light you want, and your budget. Consult with a window treatment specialist if you’re unsure.

Measure the width and height of the window frame and the depth of the window. These measurements will help you choose the right size and style for your windows.

Many window treatments come with installation instructions and can be installed by DIY enthusiasts. However, small measurement differences can cause light and filter gaps that can be avoided by a professional installation. Also, for more complex installations or custom treatments, it may be best to hire a professional.

The cleaning method depends on the type of treatment. Dusting, vacuuming, or spot cleaning are common methods. Refer to the manufacturer’s instructions for specific care.
